Why Listy?

Plenty of features

Listy includes more than just the basics.
It has plenty of power user features, including:

  • Support for batch adding tasks
  • Unlimited lists & tasks
  • Create schedules with recurring tasks
  • A timer which tracks the way you use it
Integrations

Listy integrates with Slack, IFTTT, and Zapier.

Beautiful design

Listy uses a material design framework called Materialize.
Materialize uses controls inspired by the Material Design Guidelines from Google.

Awesome stats

Listy gives you useful and in-depth information about how you're working.

Summary

To summarize, Listy offers lots of features and integrations, a nice design, and in-depth stats.
